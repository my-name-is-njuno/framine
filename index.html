About Us

Who we are

Framine Company ltd is an international leading professional services firm focused on best practice consulting and training in financial and social inclusion.

The company was founded in 2009 when the founders saw a need in privately owned healthcare institutions. It has a wealth of qualified professionals raging from finance professionals, Certified public accountants, healthcare managers and strategists

The company is geared towards providing financial solutions, advisory and training services to enhance skills and knowledge in effectively accessing financial services and re-engineering the business model to ensure profitability. It seeks to build the institutions financial muscle by leveraging on the available business opportunities and solving important problems which are pertinent in the institutions operations.
